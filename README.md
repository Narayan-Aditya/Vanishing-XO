# Vanishing-XO

Vanishing XO is a unique tic tac toe game with a twist — your oldest marks disappear after 3 moves!

Features:

- Classic tic tac toe with a vanishing mechanic
- Play with friends in 2-player mode
- Challenge the AI in solo mode
- Clean, minimal design
- Works completely offline — no internet needed
- No ads, no tracking, no in-app purchases

How to play:
Place your X or O on the board. But watch out — after your 3rd move, your oldest mark vanishes! Think ahead and outsmart your opponent in this strategic board game.

Perfect for fans of tic tac toe, noughts and crosses, XO games, and classic board games.
