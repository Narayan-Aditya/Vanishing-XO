Privacy Policy for Vanishing XO

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Introduction

Vanishing XO is a tic tac toe game developed by Aditya Narayan. This privacy policy explains how the App handles user information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App works entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
